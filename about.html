Our Mission

Social Detox, Gamified.

In a world of endless scrolling and digital distractions, we believe in reclaiming your focus and time. Actifo was born from a simple idea: what if we could make managing digital habits not just easy, but also fun and rewarding?

Our goal is to help you build a healthier relationship with technology. We've taken the principles of social detox and transformed them into an engaging, gamified experience. Through friendly competition, rewarding challenges, and insightful analytics, Actifo empowers you to take control of your screen time, reduce distractions, and focus on what truly matters.

We're not about disconnecting completely; we're about connecting more meaningfully with your own goals and with others. Join us on this journey to turn your focus into your greatest asset.
